1. Badrutt Hotel (#1 best hotel in Switzerland!)

Standard amenities include TVs, Wi-Fi access, minibars and bathtubs. The property is also home to several eateries, ranging from a casual pizzeria and grill to the gourmet Le Restaurant and La Coupole - Matsuhisa from celebrity chef Nobuyuki Matsuhisa. Another restaurant, IGNIV by Andreas Caminada, received its second Michelin star in 2020. For those traveling with little ones, Badrutt's Palace Hotel offers a supervised kids club with an outdoor play area and offers various activities, such as kid's ice skating, disco, cooking classes and even yoga and massage. Travelers say the service and attention to detail here is bar none, though many note it will cost you a pretty penny to enjoy a vacation at this hotel. Part of The Leading Hotels of the World, the property participates in the Leaders Club loyalty program.

2. Hotel d'Angleterre (#2 best hotel in Switzerland!)

Geneva's Hotel d'Angleterre houses 45 rooms and suites, all of which are styled with classic decor and original artwork. Other facets of the Hotel d'Angleterre that stand out include the Windows Restaurant – with views of the mountains framing Lake Geneva – and the Cigar Lounge at the Leopard Bar, a charming place for cigar fans to relax. Though travelers loved the restaurant's vistas, many noted the food was expensive and you could find a better meal in town. Pets are also welcome at this hotel for an additional fee; a pet concierge will assist with special walks and furry friends get some amenities of their own. Part of The Leading Hotels of the World, the property participates in the Leaders Club loyalty program.

3. Kulm Hotel St. Moritz (#3 best hotel in Switzerland!)

The property is located in St. Moritz, Switzerland, near Piz Nair mountain. The hotel's standard rooms feature large windows with views of St. Moritz Dorf or Piz Nair as well as wooden furnishings and pastel-colored fabrics. Upgraded accommodations include perks like private balconies, local wood accents and spacious salons. There are seven restaurants and five bars on-site, including a gourmet pizzeria. Travelers loved the on-site amenities, but visitors were most impressed with the hotel's activities. Winter options include skiing, traditional ice sports and bobsledding, while summers can be spent golfing, mountaineering or mountain biking. Regardless of when your adventures take place, the Kulm Spa is available to ease you into a state of relaxation before you resume the chase for adrenaline the next morning.

4. Four Seasons Hotel des Bergues Geneva (#4 best hotel in Switzerland!)

Now part of the distinctive Four Seasons brand of luxury properties, this hotel has been a staple in Geneva since 1834. The Four Seasons Hotel des Bergues sits along Lake Geneva, affording sweeping views of the cityscape and water. When it comes to accommodations, the 115 rooms and suites at this Four Seasons outpost feature high ceilings, large windows, down duvets and pillows, free internet access and LCD flat-screen TVs. Venture outside of your room to break a sweat at the 24-hour gym, relax with a treatment at the spa or go for a swim in the indoor rooftop pool. When you start to get hungry, check out the Four Seasons' three restaurants; Il Lago dishes out Italian food, the rooftop Izumi plates up Japanese-fusion fare and Le Bar Des Bergues offers light fare and innovative cocktails.


Prices at their top properties start at $1,000 per night or more...

6. Carlton Hotel St. Moritz (#6 best hotel in Switzerland!)

Built in 1913 and fully renovated in 2007, the Carlton Hotel is situated in one of the sunniest spots in the ski town of St. Moritz, Switzerland. When you're not skiing to your heart's content, spend some time in the Carlton Spa, which boasts indoor and outdoor pools, a sun terrace, a sauna, relaxation rooms and a fitness center. The hotel's staff also organizes and can help guests book activities like helicopter flights, horse and carriage rides, paragliding and cooking classes. If you're craving a midday pick-me-up, pay a visit to the Carlton Bar & Lobby for afternoon tea on the sun terrace or by the open fire. Since the Carlton Hotel St. Moritz is affiliated with The Leading Hotels of the World, Leaders Club members can earn free nights and enjoy late checkout here.
